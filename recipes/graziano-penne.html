Anthony Graziano's Penne Alla Vodka

Description

Anthony Graziano was a less well-known mobster that worked for the Bonanno crime family. His daughters made a career on the show Mob Wives and have published a family cook book.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. penne
  • 3 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 (28 oz.) cans San Marzano crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of vodka
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp. butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil
  2. Cook penne according to package instructions
  3. Drain the penne and set aside
  4. In a large saucepan over medium-high heat, heat olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic
  5. Season with salt, pepper and crushed red pepper
  6. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the onion is soft, about 10 minutes.
  7. Add the tomatoes. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer
  8. Stir occasionally for another 40 minutes.
  9. Add the vodka to the tomato mixture and continue cooking another 10 to 15 minutes
  10. Stir in the cream and the butter and cook for 10 more minutes
  11. Turn off the heat
  12. In a pasta bowl, add 2 cups sauce and toss with the pasta and ¾ cups grated cheese
  13. Sprinkle with remaining cheese
